The process

From silence to filed — in under 2 minutes

Three agents, one seamless pipeline, zero technical knowledge required.

1
🎙️ Speak in your language
Send a voice note or text on WhatsApp in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, or English. Whisper locally transcribes voice notes into text before AI processing. Agent 1 gently asks follow-up questions to extract the full case picture.
2
📚 AI finds your legal rights
Agent 2 runs a semantic search over a curated Indian legal corpus using ChromaDB semantic retrieval. It maps your case to the exact sections of PWDVA 2005, IPC/BNS 498A, POSH Act, Dowry Prohibition Act, and more.
3
📄 Documents drafted by Gemini Flash
Gemini Flash prepares a structured FIR complaint, and ReportLab generates the final PDF.
4
📬 Delivered on WhatsApp
The PDF is sent directly to the user's WhatsApp in under 90 seconds. Every document includes the nearest DLSA contact, NCW helpline, and a disclaimer to seek legal representation.
